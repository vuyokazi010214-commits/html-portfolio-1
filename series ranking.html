My Top 3 Series Of All Time

Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones has to be one of my most favourite shows. Dissapointing ending but I enjoyed the fictional historical storyline.

Vikings

Vikings is a show about the legendary Viking Ragnar Lothbrok and his family. It is based on historical events and has a lot of action and drama.

Bridgerton

Bridgerton is a period drama set in the early 19th century. It follows the lives of the Bridgerton family and their romantic entanglements. The show is known for its diverse cast and steamy romance scenes.
